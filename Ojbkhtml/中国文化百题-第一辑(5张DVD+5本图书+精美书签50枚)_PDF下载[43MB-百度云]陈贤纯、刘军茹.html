年画
年画，就是过年时张贴的画儿。
过年，也叫过春节，是中华  民族*隆重
的  传统节日。在  传统  习俗中，  新年快到来的
时候，家家户户都会把房子打扫得  干干净净的，
在大门、  窗户  上  贴上年画。
张贴  年画，既烘托了  喜洋洋的新年气氛，又
表达了主人祈求平安幸福的心愿。
年画一年  更换一次，张贴后可供一年
欣赏。
中国  年画基本上属于百姓的艺术，色彩一般
比较鲜艳、  夸张。  人们把好看的画面、幸福的
生活、  美好的  愿望  都放在了年画上。
New Year Pictures
     New Year pictures are the kind of pictures people put
up in the New Year.
     New Year here refers to the Spring Festival, the
most important holiday in China. According to traditional
customs, when the New Year is coming, every family will
clean their house and put up New Year pictures on the
doors and windows.
     These pictures not only add a happy atmosphere to
the house but also express people's pray for happiness and
safety.
     New Year pictures are changed once a year. Once
they are put up, they remain there for a whole year for
appreciation.
     Chinese New Year pictures
belong to the folk art. They
are often painted in bright and
exaggerated colors. The beautiful
pictures are the embodiment of
people's wishes for a happy life.
