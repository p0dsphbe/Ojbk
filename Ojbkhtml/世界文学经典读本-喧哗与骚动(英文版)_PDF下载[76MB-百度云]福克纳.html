作者简介

[

　　福克纳（William Faulkner）（1897-1962）， American writer and Nobel Prize laureate from Oxford， Mississippi. He is primarily known and acclaimed for his novels and short stories， many of which are set in the fictional Yoknapatawpha County， a setting Faulkner created based on Lafayette County， where he spent most of his life.

　　Faulkner was one of the most important writers in Southern literature in the United States， along with Mark Twain， Robert Penn Warren， Flannery O'Connor， Truman Capote， Eudora Welty， Thomas Wolfe， Harper Lee and Tennessee Williams.
