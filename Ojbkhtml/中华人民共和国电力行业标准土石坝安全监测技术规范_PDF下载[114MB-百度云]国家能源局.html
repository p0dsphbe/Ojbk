For a high concrete face rock-fill dam， temporary monitoring lines for the construction period shall be arranged on the top of upstream face slabs and on the cushion materials at corresponding positions of each stage. .　　3 Some monitoring points shall be arranged at the intersections between monitoring lines and monitoring sections， and additional monitoring points may be set according to dam structures， material zoning， topographical and geological conditions. The spacing ranging from 20m to 50m should be adopted for monitoring points in the dam with dam axis less than 300m， and the spacing from 50m to 100m should be adopted in the dam with dam axis longer than 300m. For the high dam constructed in a V-shape valley or the dam with steep terrain， monitoring points on the dam crest near both banks shall be densified.　　7.2.2 Arrangement of datum points and working datum points：　　1 Working datum points for a collimating line： one point shall be respectively set on the extension line on both banks of each collimating line， whose elevation should be near to that of the monitoring points and whose foundation should be rock or solid earth. In the case of broken dam axis or dam with dam axis more than 500m， additional working datum points， which may be substituted with monitoring points， may be set on the break points or in the middle of each longitudinal row of monitoring points.　　Checking datum points， which may be used for displacement correction of working datum point of a collimating line， shall be set on a solid foundation and on the extension line of the line connecting the same row of working datum points of both banks， and one or two points shall be respectively arranged on both banks. If conditions permit， horizontal deformation monitoring network or plumb line may be used for displacement measurement and correction of working datum points of the collimating line.　　2 Working datum points for the linear-angular intersection method： two working datum points should be respectively set on stable bedrock or solid soil foundation of upstream and downstream dam surface to measure their horizontal displacement. 本书特色

[

本标准规定了土石坝的安全监测项目布置、仪器设备安装埋设、仪器监测、巡视检查及资料整编分析等的要求。本标准适用于1级、2级、3级土石坝的安全监测工作，4级、5级土石坝可参照使用。等级划分按DL 5180执行。

]

内容简介

[

　　This standard specifies the requirements for monitoring items layout， installation and embedment of instruments， instrumentation monitoring， visual inspection and data compilation and analysis relating to earth and rock-fill dam safety.　　This standard is applicable to safety monitoring of earth rock-fill dams of Grades 1， 2 and 3， and may be referenced for earth rock-fill dams of Grades 4 and 5. Dam classification is in accordance with Standard DL 5180.

]
